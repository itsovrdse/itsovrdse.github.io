About Me

i'm a 21 year old guy who loves computers, music and pretty much everything that expands my creativity. the satisfaction of creating something for people to enjoy is good enough for me to keep doing what i love the most at the same time collaborating with people and working towards something you all believe in remains one of the reasons why i love doing what i do.

i used to work for a record label, creating cover arts, planning marketing strategies and interacting with the listeners, before that i was working at walmart and tim hortons. today i am pursuing a career in wed design and graphic design, i feel as if this type of creative work fits very well with what i've been doing and what i love to do.
